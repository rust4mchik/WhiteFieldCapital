Synergy. Works.

White Field Capital focuses on North American, European, and Latin American operating companies in which the firm’s principals have significant growth opportunities through the infusion of working capital. We require incumbent management teams with a clear vision for the growth of their company.

Our financing philosophy is to utilize growth capital, preferring to generate attractive returns through earnings growth and expanding the valuation multiple through building larger, more valuable enterprises with improved operations.

Mark Sawyer

Principal

Mark is the founder and principal of White Field Capital Partners. Previously, Mark was a founding member of Sheridan Legacy Group, a $150 million lower-middle market buyout firm. Mark was also Managing Director and Chief Financial Officer at Rising Tide, an alternative asset management company based in Europe. Mark has over 20 years of operations experience within various industries, including healthcare and financial services. He has served as an advisory board member for two international private equity funds, as well as multiple operating companies within the United States.

Mark earned a bachelor’s degree with honors in Accounting from Eastern Michigan University and an MBA from the University of Notre Dame, Mendoza College of Business.

Eddie Norton

Director
