About Us

Inspire. Invest. Grow.

White Field Capital is a U.S. based private equity firm with offices in Denver, Charlotte and Puerto Rico. We are focused on investing and partnering with growing businesses valued at up to $200 million.

We pride ourselves in the ability to work with others to source and structure deals that align investors, management teams and companies to reach their full potential. We are value investors who follow a disciplined approach. Our team has more than 60 years of experience and go to work every day to change the trajectory of our portfolio companies.

INVESTMENT TYPES

White Field Capital invests in invests in capital infusions into growing businesses

We believe the current LBO investment market has become over-inflated during the recent bull market and we expect opportunities in the near future to help infuse capital into high-growth businesses positioned to grow rapidly through the use of an equity infusion. Our latest fund primarily focuses on infusing growth capital into established, entrepreneur-run businesses who are at a growth inflection point within their business upon infusion of capital and execution support.

When it comes to discovering, developing, and delivering on opportunity, there’s no replacement for experience. Our leadership team has seen a broad range of the ups and downs that become realities within growing businesses, combing for more than 60 years of first hand business management experience.
